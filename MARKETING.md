# WebhookMail Marketing Plan

## SEO Strategy
- **Primary keywords:** "webhook to email", "forward webhook to email", "webhook email notification"
- **Secondary:** "webhook monitor", "webhook tester", "stripe webhook email", "github webhook email"
- **Long-tail:** "how to forward webhooks to email", "webhook to email free", "receive webhook notifications by email"
- Meta tags, canonical URL, sitemap.xml, robots.txt — all implemented

## Launch Day
1. **Hacker News** — "Show HN: WebhookMail – Forward any webhook to your email inbox"
2. **Reddit** — r/webdev, r/selfhosted, r/SideProject, r/indiehackers
3. **Product Hunt** — Schedule launch, prep tagline + screenshots
4. **Dev.to** — Blog post: "I built a webhook-to-email forwarder in a weekend"
5. **Twitter/X** — Thread about building it, tag @IndieHackers
